    How to play

    1. ★ birth and ✦ death are pinned on a borderless map, each with its year.
    2. Type to guess who lived this life. You get 6 guesses.
    3. Each wrong guess shows how far off you were — direction & distance to the real birthplace, the year gap, and any shared profession.
    4. Fewer guesses = more points. Daily is five lives, shared by everyone. Endless runs forever.
